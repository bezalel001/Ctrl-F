# Northstar Widgets IT Support FAQ

Northstar Widgets is a fictitious company used for Ctrl-F demos.

## How do I reset my password?

Employees can reset their password from the identity portal by selecting "Forgot password" and completing multi-factor authentication.

If multi-factor authentication is unavailable, contact IT Support. IT Support will verify identity before resetting account access.

## How do I request software?

Software requests must be submitted through the IT service desk. The request should include the software name, business purpose, team, cost center, and required date.

Managers must approve paid software before IT installs or assigns a license.

## What should I do if my laptop is lost or stolen?

Report a lost or stolen laptop to IT Support within 1 hour of discovery. Include the last known location and whether customer or company data may have been accessible.

IT will remotely lock or wipe managed devices when appropriate.

## What is the VPN policy?

Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems from outside an approved office network.

VPN credentials must not be shared. Repeated VPN failures should be reported to IT Support with the error message and approximate time of failure.

## Can I use personal devices?

Personal devices may be used only for email, calendar, and chat through approved managed applications. Source code, customer exports, and internal financial reports must not be stored on personal devices.

## Contacts

For access issues, hardware problems, VPN failures, or security incidents, contact IT Support.
