# Northstar Widgets HR Policy

Northstar Widgets is a fictitious company used for Ctrl-F demos.

## Work Hours

Full-time employees are expected to work 40 hours per week. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 to 15:00 in the employee's local time zone, Monday through Friday.

Employees may adjust start and end times with manager approval as long as customer commitments, team ceremonies, and support rotations are covered.

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees receive 25 paid vacation days per calendar year.

New hires receive prorated vacation days based on their start date. Unused vacation days may be carried over for up to 90 days after the end of the calendar year.

Vacation requests should be submitted in the HR portal at least 10 business days before the requested start date.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year. Employees should notify their manager as soon as practical when they are unavailable due to illness.

For absences longer than 3 consecutive business days, Human Resources may request medical documentation according to local law.

## Remote Work

Northstar Widgets supports hybrid and remote work for eligible roles. Employees must keep their work location current in the HR portal.

Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining a secure internet connection and private work environment.

## Equipment

Company laptops, monitors, and accessories are issued by IT. Employees must return company equipment within 5 business days of their final employment date.

## Contacts

Questions about benefits, leave, compensation, or employment status should be sent to Human Resources.
