# Northstar Widgets New Hire Onboarding Guide

Northstar Widgets is a fictitious company used for Ctrl-F demos.

## First Day Checklist

New hires should complete the following items on their first day:

- Sign in to the identity portal.
- Confirm access to email, calendar, chat, and the HR portal.
- Review the employee handbook.
- Join the team introduction meeting.
- Confirm shipping address for any remaining equipment.

## First Week Checklist

During the first week, new hires should:

- Complete security awareness training.
- Set up multi-factor authentication.
- Review team working agreements.
- Meet with their manager to confirm role expectations.
- Create a 30-day onboarding plan.

## Required Training

All employees must complete security awareness, data privacy, code of conduct, and workplace safety training within 10 business days of their start date.

Engineering employees must also complete secure development training before receiving production access.

## Access Requests

Access to tools and repositories should be requested through the service desk. Managers approve access based on role need.

Access requests for finance, payroll, production infrastructure, and customer data require additional approval from the system owner.

## Buddy Program

Each new hire is assigned an onboarding buddy for the first 30 days. The buddy helps answer process questions and introduces the new hire to team norms.

## Contacts

Questions about onboarding tasks should go to the hiring manager. Questions about payroll, benefits, or employee records should go to Human Resources.
